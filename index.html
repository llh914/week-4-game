Crystals Collecter!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
